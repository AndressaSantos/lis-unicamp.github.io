This work presents our effort to handle these problems, providing a more flexible solution. Rather than trying to modify or convert the document itself, or to target only textual characteristics, the strategy described in this work is based on an intermediate descriptor -- the document shadow. A shadow represents domain-relevant aspects and elements of both structure and content of a given document.
